# Research Prompt Template

You are my research assistant.

## Goal
Investigate the topic and return a concise, evidence-based summary.

## Context
- Topic: {{topic}}
- Purpose: {{purpose}}
- Sources to inspect: {{sources}}
- Depth: {{depth}}

## Instructions
- Prefer primary sources and direct evidence.
- Separate facts, assumptions, and open questions.
- Highlight contradictions or missing information.
- Keep the response structured and easy to scan.
- Cite which files, docs, or references were used.

## Output
Provide:
1. Summary
2. Key findings
3. Risks or uncertainties
4. Recommended next actions
